These SMS Terms describe how Kingpin Labs uses text messaging to communicate with you. By checking the SMS consent box on our contact form, you agree to these terms.

Program description

Kingpin Labs sends SMS messages for the following purposes only:

  • Confirming appointments or callbacks you have scheduled with us

We do not send marketing messages, promotional offers, or any communication you did not specifically request.

How to opt in

You opt in to receive SMS from Kingpin Labs by completing the following steps on our contact form:

  1. Enter your name, email address, and mobile phone number into the form
  2. Describe how we can help you in the message field
  3. Check the SMS consent checkbox, which is unchecked by default
  4. Click the Send message button to submit the form

The consent checkbox reads: "I agree to receive SMS messages from Kingpin Labs at the phone number I provided. Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies. Reply STOP to unsubscribe or HELP for assistance."

You must affirmatively check this box for your opt-in to be valid. Submitting the form without checking the box does not enroll you in SMS messaging.

How to opt out

You can opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP to any message we send you. You may also reply CANCEL, END, QUIT, or UNSUBSCRIBE — all are honored.

After you opt out, we will send one final confirmation message acknowledging your opt-out and will not send you any further SMS messages unless you opt in again through the contact form.

Help

Reply HELP or INFO to any SMS we send and you will receive a message with our contact information and a link to these terms. You can also email us at al_style@live.com for assistance.
